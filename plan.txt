2️⃣ About Tony Truman

Content example (based on LinkedIn):

Tony Truman is a hospitality entrepreneur and founding partner of IMI Group, the company behind some of Ibiza’s most iconic venues including O Beach Ibiza, Wi-Ki-Woo Hotel Ibiza, Bam Bu Ku Ibiza, and Faith Concierge.

His journey began at the age of 15 when he hosted his first party. That moment sparked a passion for events and nightlife that would shape his future.

After discovering Ibiza during a family holiday in 1983, Tony made it his mission to build some of the best hospitality experiences on the island.

Over the past 40+ years, he has built multiple businesses, hosted world-class events, and created unforgettable experiences for guests from around the globe.
